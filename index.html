Wasted Tickets

In the fast-paced Asia-Pacific region, millions of event-goers forfeit tickets yearly due to last-minute changes, leading to lost money and empty seats.

Lack of Secure Platforms

The lack of secure, user-friendly resale platforms exposes users to scams, unreliable transactions, and no buyer protection.

Fraud and Unfair Pricing

Fraudulent activities, bots, and fake accounts drive up prices and increase the risk of counterfeit tickets, undermining trust.

Fraud and Trust Issues

Fraudulent activities, counterfeit tickets, and unfair scalping practices undermine consumer trust. Bots and automated software often buy tickets in bulk, leading to inflated prices and limited access for real fans.

Lack of Transparency

Inconsistent regulation and a lack of transparency in pricing and ticket authenticity remain major concerns, especially in fragmented or unregulated markets. This creates risk and uncertainty for buyers and sellers.

Regulatory Complexity

Ticket resale is subject to varying laws and restrictions in different states and countries, with some regions banning or tightly controlling secondary sales. Navigating this requires a robust and adaptable platform.

02

Market Fragmentation

The landscape is crowded with both large players (StubHub, Viagogo) and numerous smaller, regional platforms. Our challenge is to stand out by offering a superior, more secure user experience.

03

Reputation Risks

Negative experiences due to fraud or poor customer support can quickly damage a platform’s reputation and hinder growth. We mitigate this with transparent processes and dedicated support.

Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled some of the most common questions to help you understand how Tixup works and what makes us a secure and reliable choice for your ticketing needs.

What is Tixup?

Tixup is a smart marketplace for reselling event tickets. Our platform is designed to make buying and selling tickets seamless, secure, and accessible, ensuring that changing plans never means wasted tickets and every transaction is protected from fraud.

How does Tixup prevent fraud?

We use advanced verification systems and a secure payment escrow process. Payments are held until the ticket's authenticity is confirmed, protecting both buyers and sellers. Our dedicated support team also actively monitors the platform for any suspicious activity and helps resolve disputes.

What kinds of events can I find on Tixup?

You can find tickets for a wide range of live events, including concerts, sports matches, movies, comedy shows, and more. Our platform covers both large-scale and local events to ensure you can always find a ticket for your favorite experience.

How are your fees structured?

Tixup is committed to transparency. We have a clear and competitive fee structure with no hidden costs. Our goal is to provide a fair and trustworthy marketplace for everyone, which is why we ensure all fees are communicated upfront.

How do I sell a ticket on Tixup?

Selling a ticket is effortless. Simply create an account, upload the ticket details, set your desired price, and our smart prompts will guide you through the process. Once a buyer purchases your ticket, our secure system handles the transaction and delivery.

What if there is a problem with my purchase?

We offer robust dispute resolution to protect both buyers and sellers. Our built-in system allows you to report any issues, and our dedicated support team will intervene to ensure a fair and transparent resolution. Your payment is held in escrow until the ticket authenticity is confirmed.
